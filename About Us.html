About The Company

Tail Slap PC is a computer store that prioritizes convenience, affordability, and excellent service. It start when three friends have a passion that they want to reach the goal to sell pc parts with a decent price.

 

Tail Slap PC received its name from a video game. A twist on the phrase tail slap the name was a key step in developing a brand that offered trusted services and high-quality goods. The company eventually changed its focus to offering brand new things at reasonable pricing.

 

Mission

Tail Slap PC is working hard to establish itself as a good reputation and productive Information Technology service provider with a strong commitment to customer service.

 

Vision

Tail Slap PC goal is to establish and maintain a leadership position in the rapidly increasing ICT industry by offering best quality products and services that enable our partners and customers to compete effectively utilizing best technology.
